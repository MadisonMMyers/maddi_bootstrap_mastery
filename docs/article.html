A Storm is Brewing

Captin Jack Sparrow was one of the very few who survived the Kraken trio.

Capt. Sparrow claims that they were "One tough sea cookie" and that he was fortunate that his crew made it through. His ship The Black Pearl has some pretty bad damage but is currently being prepared. Sparrow hopes to be back on the sea by next week.

"There is nothing more I like than a ye little challenge," Sparrow says, "Arg and challenge accepted."
